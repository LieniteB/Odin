Lemon bars

Back to Homepage

Description

Heathy alterantive lemon cake. Garnish with powdered suggar.

Ingredients

For crust:

  • 1 cup ground oats(1000g)
  • 1/4 cup honey (85g)
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil (50g)

For filing:

  • 5 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup honey (170g)
  • 1/2 cup emon juice (120m)
  • 1tbsp lemon zest
  • 1/3 cup ground oats (35g)

Steps

  1. For the crust: in the bowl combine the ground oats, softened coconut oil and honey.
  2. Mix with the fork unti the dough just comes together.
  3. In a lined 20 by 20cm baking pan spread the oat mixture, pressing with the fingers around the corners.
  4. Bake at 180C degrees for 10 minutes.
  5. Let it cool until you make the lemon filling.
  6. For the filling: in another bowl beat your eggs, then add honey, lemon juice, lemon zest and ground oats.
  7. Mix until wel combined.
  8. Pour the egg mixture over the oatmeal crust and bake at 180C degrees for 20-25 minutes, unti set but soft when gently touched.
  9. Let it cool for an hour at room temperature, then place the baking pan into the fridge for 4-5 hours.
