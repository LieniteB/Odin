Nutella

Back to Homepage

Description

Nutty homemade nutella.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rosted hazelnuts (130g)
  • 1/4 cup pitted dates or date past (75g)
  • 1/4 dark chocolate (50g)
  • 1 tbsp unsweetened cocoa
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup or honey
  • 2-3 tbsp coconut cream, coconut mik or heavy-whipping cream

Steps

  1. In a food procesor add the rosted hazlenuts and blend until the oils from the nuts start to release.
  2. Now, add the dates, melted dark chocolate, cocoa powder, maple syrup and coconut cream.
  3. Process unti it's smooth to your liking.
